MonoMuse


Welcome to MonoMuse

MonoMuse is a museum dedicated to the colorful world of Sanrio — celebrating the characters, stories, and kawaii culture that have brought joy to fans across generations and around the globe.

From Hello Kitty's debut on a tiny vinyl coin purse in 1974 to a cast of over 450 beloved characters, Sanrio has created a universe built on friendship, kindness, and the enduring power of cuteness. MonoMuse brings this world to life through immersive exhibitions, rare archival merchandise, original artwork, and interactive experiences designed for visitors of all ages. Whether you grew up collecting My Melody stationery or discovered Cinnamoroll last week, there is something here for every fan.

Plan your visit to experience our rotating Sanrio exhibitions, character meet-and-greet events, and hands-on activities designed for all ages!
