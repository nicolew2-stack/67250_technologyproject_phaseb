Sanrio History & Experience

Founded in 1960 by Shintaro Tsuji, Sanrio began as the Yamanashi Silk Center before pivoting to create cute, character-driven merchandise that sparked the global "kawaii" (cute) phenomenon. The company realized early on that adding cute designs to everyday items significantly boosted sales, leading to the creation of their most iconic character, Hello Kitty, in 1974.


Today, Sanrio's rich design history and cultural impact are celebrated in immersive museum spaces and exhibitions worldwide. MonoMuse is proud to bring that same spirit of warmth, nostalgia, and kawaii joy to our visitors — through rotating galleries, rare archival displays, and the beloved characters that have defined generations of fans across the globe.
